Some of my projects

Taskwal

Web Application

Taskwal

I made this app for spring Mhacks 2014. The site was inspired by a friend and I when we both craved burritos, but we were too lazy to walk all the way to the restaurant. On top of this, the restaurant did not do delivery. We created a marketplace for tasks, where students could interact with each other and pay other students to complete tasks for them, such as bringing a burrito to their dorm room. The site integrated Venmo for easy payments and Twilio for mobile alerts and verifications.
