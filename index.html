Yelp Sentiment Analysis

For this project, I used the dataset from the Yelp Dataset Challenge (http://www.yelp.com/dataset_challenge/) to compute and rank words according to positivity/negativity scores. To compute the scores, I used the following formula: Positivity(w) = log P(w in PositiveReviews) – log P(w in AllReviews), where P(·) denotes probability and log(x) is the natural logarithm of x. To run the project, I wrote scripts in Spark and ran the jobs on a University of Michigan ARC Hadoop cluster.

Python, Hadoop, Spark, Linux
